You are probably here because of one of two reasons: either you were in the same Zoom group as me and are following up on the link I provided when asked for a fun fact; or, you are here because you chose to investigate the potential existence of a website dedicated to one Matthew E. Vanaman. In either case, you arrived here at your own volition to check out my website. Bless you.

Here some interesting (?) facts about me:

I used to race motocross, and we had a dirt bike track in our back yard. It was sweet indeed. Unfortunately my glory days were swiftly absconded with by a severely broken leg requiring airlift out of my back yard via helicopter. I peaked too early, as they say, and have only been on a bike once since. I still follow the sport closely, out of nostalgia and to maintain my blue-collar street cred among my ivory-tower colleagues.

Although possessing many of the quintessential traits of the head-in-the-clouds academic, I was in two college musicals . My solo was Pinball Wizard by The Who. For one moment, the light of the rock gods was briefly shown upon me.

It’s all downhill from here, really. I definitely , definitely peaked too early.

I now find myself trapped with a hipster roommate who pays rent, eats for free, never cleans her bathroom, and needs. It hurts, how much she needs. And she’s hyperactive with no regard
for personal space. We’re all having a good time (mostly her).
